About Dr. Imran Ali Rizvi

Specialization: Paediatrics

Having completed both his MBBS and DCH Paediatrics from Mumbai, Dr.Imran is a hardworking and dedicated Mumbai based paediatrician who specialises in various disorders related to child health. He strongly believes in practicing ethical medicine and works on the principle of evidence based medicine for children. He is currently a Consultant Paediatrician at Ace Hospital, Mira Road, ZRS Hospital, Kurla and KJ Cure Hospital, Jogeshwari. He also provides online consultations on the popular online platform - Our Little Joys.

Services Offered



    We offer a variety of paediatric services at our clinics to infants, children and adolescents. These include:

    1.Well-child visits: Providing regular check-ups and immunizations to ensure the healthy growth and development of children.

    2.Sick visits: Providing medical care for a range of acute illnesses and chronic conditions related to respiratory, neurological, gastrointestinal, renal systems or connective tissue disorders.

    3.Developmental and behavioral screenings: Providing screenings for behavioral issues, such as autism or ADHD.
